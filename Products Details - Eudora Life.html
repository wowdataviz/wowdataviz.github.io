Description:

    Advanced glycation end products (AGEs) accumulate with age and are key indicators of aging and disease. They disrupt mitochondrial function and block cellular energy production; they accelerate free radical accumulation and reduce the production of antioxidant enzymes; they cause protein cross-linking and degeneration in normal tissue, resulting in the aging and abnormality of mitochondria, blood vessels, bones, skin, eyes, liver and lungs.

    Anti-AGEs Enhanced Formula Softgels - It can comprehensively prevent AGEs damage and boost cell vitality.

1. Inhibit the formation of AGEs

    AGEs are the end products of nonenzymatic glycation, which are reversible in the early stages, whereas the advanced glycation end products are very stable once they are formed.
    AGEs inhibitors act on the entire process of nonenzymatic glycation to prevent the production of glycated toxins, AGEs. The AGEs inhibitors block the production of early glycation products, reduce and eliminate intermediates in glycation reactions and prevent the highly reversible early glycation products from the formation of irreversible end products.

2. Resist oxidation and reduce AGEs

    The process of glycosylation is accompanied by the occurrence of oxidation. Studies have shown that antioxidants also have an inhibitory effect on the production of AGEs.
    Antioxidants are involved in the process of oxidation to inhibit the formation of AGEs. Free radicals and peroxidation play an important role in the formation of AGEs. Good antioxidants inhibit glycation and reduce the production of AGEs.

3. Accelerate the degradation of AGEs

    With increasing age, AGEs production and accumulation of glycated toxins in serum and tissues are inevitable. The advanced glycation end products (AGEs) in the body are partially degraded by mononuclear macrophages, and the AGEs polypeptides formed by degradation are excreted through the kidney.
    The immune enhancer can promote the activity of monocyte macrophage and accelerate the degradation and excretion of AGEs glycosylated toxin.

4. Reduce AGEs damages

    Advanced glycation end products (AGEs) can cause damage to cells, mitochondria, and healthy tissues. The end products formed the glycosylated toxins, which are attached to healthy organs, lead to cross-linking of the originally ordered molecules, destroy the normal cell structure and mitochondrial function, become the cause of disease and aging.
    Cytoprotective agents reduce AGEs damage to cells, mitochondria, normal proteins and tissues, protecting the body from injury.

5. Enhance cell energy

    Mitochondria are the energy factories of cells. Ninety percent of the energy required by a cell comes from mitochondrial oxidative phosphorylation. AGEs disrupt the energy supply of mitochondria and block the production of cellular energy, which become the important cause of the aging process.
    Energy booster can increase energy supply in the mitochondria, reverse the AGEs-induced energy production decrease in mitochondria, restore the normal function of mitochondria, enhance cell viability, and make the series of products more effective.
